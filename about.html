About Us

Ayam Gempuk AG was founded with a passion for serving the crispiest, most flavorful fried chicken in town. Our secret recipe, handed down through generations, combines fresh herbs and spices to create a taste experience like no other.

From our humble beginnings as a small food stall, we have grown into a beloved brand that values quality, warmth, and authenticity. Every dish is prepared with care, using only the finest ingredients.

Whether you're dining in, ordering online, or visiting our food truck, we promise an unforgettable culinary experience with every bite. Welcome to the Ayam Gempuk family!

To deliver the most delicious, crispy, and memorable ayam goreng experience, using fresh ingredients and authentic recipes that bring joy to every customer.

To become the top choice for fried chicken lovers across Malaysia and Southeast Asia, known for quality, consistency, and community care.
